Custom Robot Arm


Background


Robotics in the manufacturing industry is something I am very interested in pursuing. In order to gain a fundamental understanding of the topic, I decided to build a basic 5-DOF robot arm to begin experimenting with. I am following some basic templates but plan on taking the project further with the added power of Raspberry Pi and ultrasonic sensors. This is an ongoing project, and I will be updating this page as the project progresses.
